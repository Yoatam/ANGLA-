About Our Restaurant

We are a locally owned and operated burger restaurant that has been serving up delicious, high-quality burgers in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia since 2008.
At ANGLA Burger, our mission is to provide our customers with the best possible burger experience. We use only the freshest and highest-quality ingredients, and our burgers are cooked to perfection every time. We believe that our burgers should be not only delicious, but also healthy and affordable.
Our vision is to become the go-to destination for burgers in Addis Ababa and beyond. We strive to provide an enjoyable and welcoming atmosphere for all of our customers, and we are dedicated to constantly improving our menu and service to ensure that every customer leaves satisfied.
We currently have four locations in Addis Ababa, each one offering the same great food and service that our customers have come to expect from ANGLA Burger. Whether you're in the mood for a classic burger or something a little more exotic, we have something to suit every taste.
So come on down to ANGLA Burger and see for yourself why we're the best burger joint in town! We look forward to serving you soon.
